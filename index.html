The art of teaching

I am an artist, my art is teaching and my canvas are the wonderful brains of the children I teach. In addition to my professional degree, I have attended different diplomas, trainings and I do a lot of reading and research because I am always eager to constantly learn, study and think about better methods of teaching to children everything they need and enjoy it at the same time, because the best way in which they can learn something is by doing it and doing it with pleasure, only in this way can they make little people with exceptional abilities.
